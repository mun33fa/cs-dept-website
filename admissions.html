How to Apply

Application Steps

01

Visit Admissions Portal

Go to admissions.comsats.edu.pk and create a new applicant account using your CNIC and valid email address.

02

Fill Online Application Form

Complete the online application form with personal information, academic history, and preferred program of study.

03

Upload Required Documents

Upload scanned copies of Matriculation certificate, Intermediate/degree transcripts, CNIC/B-Form, and passport-size photograph.

04

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application processing fee of PKR 1,500 via bank challan or online payment gateway.

05

Appear in Entry Test

Sit for the COMSATS Entry Test or submit valid NTS/GAT scores as required for your program.

06

Merit List & Enrollment

Check the merit list on the admissions portal. If selected, complete fee payment and enrollment within the specified deadline.
